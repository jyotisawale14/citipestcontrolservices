Services

We offer a range of pest control services to meet your needs, including:

Cockroach Pest Control

Effective cockroach control is important to prevent the spread of diseases, maintain a healthy and hygienic environment, and minimize annoyance caused by cockroaches.

Ants Pest Control

The treatment include using bait stations, applying insecticides, and sealing entry points to prevent ants from entering the property.

Spider And Lizard Pest Control

The treatment include sealing entry points, reducing clutter and debris, and using insecticides or natural repellents to deter spiders and lizards.

Mosquitoes Pest Control

The treatment may include eliminating standing water sources, using insecticides, and installing mosquito traps or screens to prevent mosquitoes from entering the property.

Fruit Flies Pest Control

The treatment include eliminating sources of attraction, such as ripened or fermenting fruits, using fly traps or baits, and applying insecticides.

Rodent Control Treatment:

a process that involves identifying and eliminating rodents, such as rats and mice, from a specific area or property.

Covid Disinfection Treatment:

Proper COVID-19 disinfection treatment is important to help prevent the spread of the virus and to maintain a safe and healthy environment for individuals in the affected area.

Bed bug control:

Bed bugs can be a nightmare to deal with, but we have the tools and expertise to eliminate them for good.

Anti Termite Treatment:

Effective anti-termite treatment is important to prevent structural damage and loss of property value associated with termite infestations.

Fly Control Treatment

The treatment include removing or treating breeding sites such as garbage and sewage, using fly traps or baits, and applying insecticides to affected areas .

Honeybee Removal Treatment

It's important to use caution when removing honeybee colonies, as they play a vital role in pollinating crops and maintaining ecosystems. .

Powderpost Beetle Treatment

The treatment include using insecticides, fumigation to eradicate the beetles in all stages of their life cycle, including eggs, larvae, and adults. .
